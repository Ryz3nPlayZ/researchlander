Frequently asked questions

How does Researcana prevent hallucinations?
Every claim is verified by a dedicated agent against actual source documents. Citations are injected at the token level. Click any citation to see the exact passage.
Is my research data private?
Absolutely. Your documents and queries are never used to train our models. We offer institutional deployments for on-premise requirements.
Which citation styles are supported?
APA, MLA, Chicago, Harvard, IEEE, Vancouver, and Nature. Custom styles available. All citations include DOI links.
